Some of our projects

Community impact

At Rene Fashions,we believe in using our creativity to make a difference.Recently,we supported refugee women in Nakivale Refugee Settlement by providing them with clothing to restore confidence and dignity.We are committed to empowering women not only through fashion but also through compassion.Seeing their smiles reminded me why i started Rene Fashions.

"As refugees,life is not easy,but today,we feel loved and remembered.All thanks to Rene Fashions."-One of the refugees that received clothes.

Prom party project

Rene Fashions provided custom dresses and styling for young ladies attending their prom at Seeta High School.Each outfit was carefully designed to match the individual's personality,style and comfort,ensuring they felt confident and radiant.This project was a celebration of elegance,creativity and the joy of fashion,making prom night memorable for each participant.

"I felt like a queen thanks to Rene Fashions!My prom dress was perfect,and i couldn't stop smiling all night."-Our Prom Queen.

Fashion showcase project

Rene Fashions proudly organised a clothing showcase to present our latest designs.The event highlighted our creativity and passion for modern fashion.Each piece was carefully designed to reflect elegance,confidence and cultural pride.
